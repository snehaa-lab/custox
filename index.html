About Us

Our Custox app allows people to get notifed about their favourite T-shirts available in shops in their locality. It's benefical for the people who likes in shop shopping but don't know in which shop their prefered cloth will be,so Custox saves their time and energy. Customers can also share their product preferences and get notified when matching items are available in any shop

Shopkeepers can easily manage their inventory and do customer segmentation analysis of their customers. This builds a strong connection between local brands and their customers while promoting sustainable fashion practices.
